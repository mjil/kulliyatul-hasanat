Welcome to Kulliyatul Hasanat

Empowering individuals through skill-based education in Coding, Quranic Studies, Arabic, Urdu, Handicrafts, and Hijama.

About Us

Kulliyatul Hasanat is a vocational training institute offering expert-led courses in various fields. Our mission is to equip individuals with practical skills for a successful career.

Our Courses

  • Computer Training (Coding & AI): Learn programming, web development, and AI applications.
  • Quranic Studies (Tajweed & Tafsir): Master the proper recitation of the Quran and understand its interpretation.
  • Arabic Language (Conversational & Quranic): Develop fluency in Arabic for communication and religious studies.
  • Urdu Language (Poetry & Literature): Explore the beauty of Urdu poetry and classical literature.
  • Handicrafts & Tailoring: Gain skills in sewing, embroidery, and handmade crafts.
  • Hijama (Cupping Therapy): Learn the techniques and benefits of traditional Islamic medicine.
